WoodPeckeriOS，
使用WoodPeckeriOS 1.2.9版本的動態庫，
動態庫若更新請自己替换"Library/MobileSubstrate/DynamicLibraries/WoodPeckeriOS.framework"。


Supported devices

  • iOS12~iOS13.5
  • A7-A13
  • unc0ver

Uninstall

  1. just uninstall from Cydia
  2. reboot (don't forget!)
